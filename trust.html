Visibility Protection Rules
Fair Ranking
What does not reduce visibility?
  • Temporary short breaks
  • Low posting frequency if accuracy remains high
  • New experts in probation who are still being assessed
What does reduce visibility?
  • Extended inactivity without updates
  • Confirmed manipulation attempts
  • Repeated low-value or misleading content
  • Unresolved trust or moderation issues
Why this matters
Trust is the foundation of the ExpertMarket economy. Verification protects users from fake experts, proof-of-knowledge raises content quality, anti-fraud checks defend the market from manipulation, and transparent score logic makes pricing and visibility easier to trust.
