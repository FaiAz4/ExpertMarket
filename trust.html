Inactivity and Visibility
Ranking Rules
1

0–14 Days Inactive

Experts remain fully visible across recommendation and ranking surfaces.

2

15–30 Days Inactive

Visibility begins to fall in trending modules and category recommendations.

3

31–60 Days Inactive

Experts lose active ranking strength and appear lower in discovery pages.

4

60+ Days Inactive

Profiles stay on the platform but are archived from top discovery until reactivated.

Moderation Principles
Fairness
Visibility is reduced when:
  • There is extended inactivity
  • Manipulation is detected
  • Misleading or low-quality content is repeated
  • Trust and safety reviews remain unresolved
Visibility is protected when:
  • Experts take short breaks
  • Posting frequency is low but quality stays high
  • New experts are still within the probation window
Why this matters
Trust is the core of ExpertMarket. Verification protects users from fake experts, proof-of-knowledge improves quality, anti-fraud checks preserve pricing integrity, and visibility rules ensure the market rewards reliable participation rather than manipulation.
